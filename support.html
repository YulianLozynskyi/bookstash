Frequently asked questions

How do I add a book by URL?

Tap the "+" button in your library, paste the URL of any book page. BookStash will automatically fetch the title, author, and cover.

Does my library sync across devices?

Yes. Your library is stored in the cloud and available on any device where you're signed into your BookStash account.

How do I change a book's status?

Open the book and tap the current status (Want to read / Reading / Read) to cycle through. Or long-press a book card to pick any status directly.

How do I create a cover collage?

Go to the Stats tab, select Collage, customize the settings, and save the image to your photo library.

I forgot my password. What do I do?

On the sign-in screen, tap "Sign in with code" ‚Äî we'll send a one-time code to your email address.

The app doesn't recognize a book URL

Some websites are not supported yet. Try a link from a different source or enter the details manually. If you think a site should be supported, let us know.
